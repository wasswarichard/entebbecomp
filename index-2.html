Upcoming Events

SCIENCE TRIP

curriculum based school science trip that provide students with experiences outside their everyday activities, develop a lifelong interest in science, nature, and the environment which provide fantastic opportunities for our students to learn in exciting, dynamic and challenging environments.

SCRIPTURE UNION

At ECSS scripture Union of Uganda, we nurture children and youth holistically, helping people engage with God through the Bible so that they may follow Jesus Christ and be instrumental in transforming Africa.

ATHLETICS

At ECSS, athletics is an activity that's done to boost the students thinking where a collection of sporting events that involve competitive running, jumping, throwing, and walking are carried out.

ECSS LIBRARY

The school library that is integral to the teaching and learning process. The school library facilitates the work of the classroom teacher and ensures our students have equitable access to resources, irrespective of home opportunities or constraints.
