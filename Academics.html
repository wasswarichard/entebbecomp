CURRICULUM
 Entebbe Comprehesive Secondary school  curriculum is distinctive in it breadth and academic rigour. It is an imaginative and well balanced curriculum tailored to the needs of each student individually. This is manifested in the optional subjects like Computer studies, Home economics, Agriculture etc which students take at will. We provide both sciences and Arts at ordinary and Advanced level. They are all taught respectively and the students are encouraged to excel in their specialties.

ADMISSION

O'LEVEL

A maximum of 28 aggregates obtained from primary leaving examinations will enable you join Entebbe Comprehensive Secondary school community. For continuing students two reports and a recommendation from the previous school are a requirement. Subjects offered at ECSS include Biology, Physcics, Chemistry,Mathematics, Literature, History, commerce, Enterpreneurship, technical drawing, CRE and IRE, Luganda and Fine Art.

A'LEVEL

A maximum of credits in the preferred subjects of study will enable you join Entebbe Comprehensive Secondary School commun ity. Subjects offered at ECSS include Biology, Physcics, Chemistry,Mathematics, Literature, History, Divinity/CRE and IRE, Luganda and Fine Art.
