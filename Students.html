The Origin of Entebbe Comprehensive Secondary School 

Entebbe Comprehensive Secondary School is a government-aided institution, which is located in Division 'B' of Entebbe Municipality in Waskiso district.  Following the closure of Entebbe Teachers' training college in 2001, Entebbe Comprehensive Secondary School emerged and took over the premises. It offers ordinary and advanced level programmes based on the syllabus issued by the National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC) To date, the school moto is 'Knowledge is a treasure'. What was interesting about its progress was the support of the parents and the community they lobbied the government to post teachers and erect more structures. The vision of Entebbe Comprehensive Secondary School expressed clearly in 2016 is "To probe morally upright slef reliant, versatile and patriotic citizens"
The theme of this year is to "seek, learn and master'
